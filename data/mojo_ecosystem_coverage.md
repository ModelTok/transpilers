## Verdict

**Mojo's ecosystem is compute-heavy and infrastructure-light:** deep coverage of
numerics, tensors, LLM/ML and GPU kernels (driven by Modular's own stdlib + a long
tail of model ports), but the "boring glue" that real applications depend on —
pydantic-style validation, ORM/DB drivers, templating, plotting, logging,
compression, a true test framework — is essentially greenfield, making those the
highest-leverage libraries to bootstrap.
